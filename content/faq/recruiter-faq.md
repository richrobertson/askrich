Yes. Rich led modernization work spanning runtime upgrades, platform transitions, dependency rationalization, and security/compliance improvements across large-scale deployments.

## Can Rich lead while still being hands-on?

Yes. His background combines architecture direction, implementation ownership, code quality mentorship, and cross-team technical alignment.

## What environments does Rich perform best in?

Teams facing ambiguous requirements, legacy complexity, or high-accountability delivery timelines where technical depth and practical execution both matter.

Both. He has delivered in enterprise consulting contexts and in product/platform organizations, including customer-facing systems at scale.
