## How to Use This Group

Use these answers to establish a consistent narrative at the start and end of interviews. Keep them concise and adapt details to the role context.

## Tell me about yourself.

Strategic answer:
I am a senior software engineer focused on backend and platform systems, with 15+ years across Oracle, Starbucks, Slalom, and Avanade. The center of my work is modernization and migration in high-risk environments, where reliability and delivery speed both matter. Recent examples include leading Oracle CNS migration strategy in OCI and driving RQS modernization work that reduced security compliance issues by about 60%. I am strongest when I can connect architecture decisions directly to business outcomes, mentor teams through execution, and leave systems more maintainable than I found them.

## Describe your leadership style.

Strategic answer:
My style is technical, calm, and outcome-oriented. I lead with clarity on tradeoffs, raise risks early, and hold a high quality bar while respecting delivery constraints. I am most effective in environments where teams need both deep engineering judgment and practical execution.

## Why should we hire you for a senior/staff backend role?

Strategic answer:
I bring a combination that is hard to find in one profile: deep backend/platform engineering, proven modernization execution in complex organizations, and consistent leadership in ambiguous, high-stakes work. I can help teams make better technical decisions, deliver reliably under pressure, and improve long-term maintainability without slowing the business. That mix tends to produce durable results, not just short-term wins.
