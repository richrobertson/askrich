## Positioning

Rich combines deep backend/platform engineering with strong execution in complex real-world environments.

He is especially valuable when teams need someone who can:
- turn ambiguous requirements into an actionable delivery plan,
- make sound technical tradeoffs under production constraints,
- modernize legacy systems without disrupting business-critical workflows,
- lead through influence, mentoring, and cross-functional communication.

**Bottom line:** Rich consistently delivers resilient systems and practical business outcomes in high-ambiguity, high-accountability contexts.
